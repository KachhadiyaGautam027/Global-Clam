Our Journey
Global Glam, a venture of Bitech Smart Solutions Pvt. Ltd., is a premium exporter from India. We specialize in customized embroidered clothing materials, sequin embroidered materials, and heavy design works.

Our journey started in 2005 with a vision to bring high-quality clothing materials to the world. We have a team of experienced designers and craftsmen who are passionate about creating unique and exquisite designs.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us build a loyal customer base across the globe.
